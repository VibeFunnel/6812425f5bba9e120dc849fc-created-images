Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EtherMail

What's the new update Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) about?

Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) transforms your email into a secure Web3 wallet. It allows you to send, receive, and manage digital assets directly through your email interface, eliminating the need for separate wallet applications. This makes blockchain technology accessible to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.

How can I create my Email-as-a-Wallet?

Simply sign up for EtherMail using your Gmail or Apple ID. Our system automatically generates a secure wallet that's linked to your email address, with no complicated setup process required. The entire process takes less than 2 minutes, and you'll be ready to start sending and receiving crypto immediately.

How does EtherMail's Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) technology work with other Web3 products?

EtherMail's EaaW technology is designed to be fully compatible with existing Web3 infrastructure. You can interact with dApps, NFT marketplaces, and DeFi protocols directly through your email interface. Our system translates complex blockchain interactions into simple email commands, making Web3 accessible to everyone.
