Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. If you don't see what you're looking for, reach out to our support team.

What's the new update Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) about?

Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) transforms your email into a fully functional Web3 wallet, allowing you to send, receive, and manage crypto assets directly through your inbox without needing a separate wallet application.

How can I create my Email-as-a-Wallet?

Simply sign up for EtherMail using your existing email address or social login. Your Email-as-a-Wallet is automatically created and ready to use immediately with no technical setup required.

How does EtherMail's Email-as-a-Wallet (EaaW) technology work with other Web3 products?

EtherMail's EaaW technology is designed to seamlessly integrate with existing Web3 products, protocols, and dApps. You can interact with DeFi platforms, NFT marketplaces, and other blockchain applications directly through your email interface.

Is EtherMail secure? How do you protect my assets?

EtherMail employs bank-grade security protocols including two-factor authentication, biometric verification, and advanced encryption. Your private keys are never stored on our servers and all transactions require explicit approval through multiple verification steps.

What blockchain networks does EtherMail support?

EtherMail currently supports Ethereum, Polygon, Binance Smart Chain, Solana, and Avalanche with more networks being added regularly. Our goal is to provide a unified interface for all major blockchain networks.
